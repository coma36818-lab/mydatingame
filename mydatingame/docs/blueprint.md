# **App Name**: Influencer Edge

## Core Features:

- AI Trend Analyzer: Uses AI to analyze social media trends and suggest relevant content ideas to users, taking into consideration current hot topics, ideal posting times and competitor content. Uses the reasoning of an LLM tool to incorporate information, as necessary.
- Profile Analyzer: Analyzes user's social media profiles (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) and provides insights on follower growth, engagement rate, and best posting times.
- Content Calendar: Allows users to plan and schedule their social media content in advance, based on AI-driven insights.
- Affiliate Program Tracker: Aggregates various affiliate programs relevant to content creators, allowing users to discover and manage their affiliate partnerships.
- Content Submission: Allows creators to easily submit their content for potential publication on MyDatingameâ€™s platform.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep magenta (#C72183) to reflect creativity, digital artistry, and social influence.
- Background color: Dark navy blue (#1A202C) to ensure contrast and make content pop, signifying a 'night mode' aesthetic suitable for content consumption and creation, often done in the late hours.
- Accent color: Electric violet (#8F50D7) to highlight key actions and interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif for a modern, bold digital feel, optimized for headings and important text.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif for excellent readability and clean appearance in articles, tips, and descriptions.
- Use vibrant, minimalist icons representing various social media platforms, trends, and tools; the icons should use the accent color.
- Subtle, modern transitions and animations to create a dynamic and engaging user experience.
